Welcome to Eagle's Nest!

Hi! My name is GustoniaEagle, but Eagle will do. I am a mod developer for Minecraft, using the Forge API. I am currently working on the Adventurer's Amulet mod, and am hoping for a release in the very close future. To keep up with the updates, either follow me on GitHub, or follow me on Twitter.

GustoCraft

GustoCraft is a modded server, ran by myself for invited members of the community. You may see my live stream things from the server on Twitch. You may even get a sneak peak at upcoming features.

OpenSource

All of my code is open source, and that is the way it will always be. I am still learning Java, and I learned most of what I know from other GitHub users, as well as some online tutorials. If you spot somewhere I have made a mistake, or could do it better, let me know by either sending me a message or submitting a Pull Request.

CurseForge

All files for Adventurer's Amulets will be hosted on CurseForge. A link to the site will be posted here as soon as I release the mod to the public.

Support or Contact

If you have any queries, e-mail me on gustoniaeagle@gmail.com, message me on Twitter or send me a message on GitHub. If you find a bug, submit it as an issue on the repository with full description and crash reports.
